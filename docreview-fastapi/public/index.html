About DocReview

Transparent Healthcare at Graphic Era Hospital

DocReview is the official patient review platform exclusively for Graphic Era Hospital — empowering patients to make confident healthcare decisions through genuine, verified feedback.

100% Exclusive to Graphic Era Hospital All doctors listed are verified staff of Graphic Era Hospital. No external listings, ever.
JWT-Secured Authentication Patient data and reviews are protected with industry-standard JSON Web Token security.
One Review Per Doctor Policy Each registered patient can submit exactly one honest review per doctor — ensuring fairness.
Admin Moderation All reviews pass through hospital admin moderation before publication.
Platform Features

Everything You Need to Choose the Right Doctor

DocReview brings transparency and trust to your healthcare journey at Graphic Era Hospital.

Browse by Department

Explore all specialist departments at Graphic Era Hospital — Cardiology, Neurology, Orthopedics, Pediatrics, and more — to find the right expert.

Verified Patient Reviews

Only registered, authenticated patients of Graphic Era Hospital can post reviews — ensuring every rating you read is genuine and trustworthy.

1–5 Star Doctor Ratings

Rate your experience with any doctor using our clear 1–5 star system, with a detailed feedback section to share your full experience.

JWT Secure Authentication

Your account is protected by JSON Web Token authentication, ensuring that only you can manage your profile and review submissions.

One Review Per Doctor

Each patient may submit one review per doctor. This prevents spam and maintains a fair, unbiased rating environment for everyone.

Admin Review Moderation

Hospital administrators review and approve all submissions before publication, maintaining the integrity of the platform and patient trust.
